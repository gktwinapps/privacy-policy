Finance Tracker

Track your investments and financial status with ease and privacy.

Finance Tracker helps you monitor all your assets in one secure place — with full privacy. Whether you're managing investments in India or the US, this app makes it simple and safe.

🔒 Privacy

  • Fingerprint authentication for secure access
  • Your data is stored only on your device or Google Drive
  • No external servers or tracking
  • Full control over your financial records

📊 Supported Asset Types

  • Fixed Deposits (FD)
  • Recurring Deposits (RD)
  • Bonds
  • Systematic Investment Plans (SIP)
  • Insurance Policies
  • Others

🔁 Smart Backup & Import

  • Export your data anytime as a backup
  • Import or sync with Google Drive
  • Backup and restore via Google Drive
  • Never lose your financial history again

🎯 Ideal Allocation & Tracking

  • Define your ideal asset allocation
  • View your actual vs. target portfolio distribution
  • Easily rebalance and plan your financial goals
